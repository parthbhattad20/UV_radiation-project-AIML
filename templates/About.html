About Us

Welcome to our collaborative project between Vishwaniketan Institute and Avignon University, dedicated to understanding the impact of UV rays on living organisms. Our partnership combines expertise in biology, environmental science, and innovative research methods to explore the effects of ultraviolet radiation on various life forms.


Our team consists of Vaibhav Bondar, Sharath Pai, and Purushottam Habib, who bring diverse skills and a shared passion for scientific inquiry. Under the expert guidance of Dr. Seyed Jazayeri, Professor of Bioinformatics at Avignon University, and Prof. Abderrahim Benslimane, Professor of Computer Sciences at Avignon University, we are well-equipped to tackle the complexities of this research.


At Vishwaniketan Institute, we are committed to advancing scientific knowledge and promoting environmental sustainability. Our collaboration with Avignon University, a leading institution in research and education, enables us to leverage a wealth of academic resources and cutting-edge technology.


Our research aims to uncover the complex interactions between UV radiation and biological systems, providing valuable insights into the potential risks and benefits associated with UV exposure. By studying a diverse range of organisms, from microorganisms to plants and animals, we strive to develop comprehensive strategies for mitigating adverse effects and harnessing the positive aspects of UV light.


Together with Avignon University, we are dedicated to fostering scientific innovation, promoting public awareness, and contributing to the global body of knowledge on environmental health and safety. Join us on this journey to explore the fascinating world of UV rays and their impact on life on Earth.
